Participation Information Sheet and Informed Consent Form

Hello, I am Tanira Shaikh, pursuing my doctor of philosophy (Ph. D.) in Psychology from Amity Institute of Psychology and Allied Sciences at Amity University Kolkata.

This informed consent form is for general population between the age group of 21-35 years, who use generative artificial intelligence (Gen-AI) in their daily lives. You are invited to participate in this research exploring human-AI interaction and conversation satisfaction.

The purpose of this research is to discover people's inclination towards using artificial intelligence tools for conversation satisfaction, especially those who feel rejected in social circumstances and understand conversation satisfaction from their point of view. It is also to collect and analyse typing and touch interaction patterns to predict association between individual's interaction patterns, perceived feelings of rejection, and personality traits.

In this research, you will interact with a touch-screen interface by filling a standardized survey questionnaire. The total duration will be 35 to 40 minutes. No direct identifiers such as name, phone number, email address, or precise location will be collected or used in any manner.

All your personal data will be kept confidential and you will remain completely anonymous. Your participation is completely voluntary and you may withdraw at any time.
